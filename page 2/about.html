Our Manifesto

We Believe In Restorative Travel

Common Inquiries

Quick answers about booking, customization, and cancellation.

Are flights included in the booking price?
No, our booking price covers all boutique local lodging, professional guides, entry fees, and ground transfers. Flights to and from the starting airport are arranged by travelers.
Can I modify my travel dates after purchase?
Yes. You can request date adjustments up to 14 days prior to departure at no extra cost, subject to sanctuary room availability.
What is the group size limit for your excursions?
To ensure absolute peace and premium quality, all our voyages are capped at a maximum of 12 passengers per guide. Private custom guides can also be reserved.
